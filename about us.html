About Us

PerfectTea, LLC was founded in 2019 by Lenard Estacio. Based in Brooklyn, NY, the company is a health and flavor-driven brand that produces quality tea bags for everyday consumption. As a wellness brand, the company creates products ranging from tea flavors such as chamomile, green, and black, aimed at consumers between the ages of 18 to 90. The company strives to provide our customers with a blend of organic herbs to ensure that they get the most out of our product. With a variety of herbs and flavors, customers are guaranteed to find tea flavors that are perfect for them. We hope to establish a long-lasting relationship with our customers and in return, we’ll continue to give them the best tea they’ve ever tasted. Quality tea made with fresh organic herbs and tea leaves produced allows our tea to stay both pure and natural. Whether it is for health or just as a pure enjoyment, our tea will leave our customers satisfied.
